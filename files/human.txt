THE BERKELEY JUGGLING CLUB

We are a community-run juggling and circus arts community based in Berkeley, California.

WHAT WE DO
Weekly juggling meetings open to all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced jugglers.
Shared practice space for juggling, flow arts, diabolo, unicycling, and related circus skills.
Occasional workshops, games, and informal skill-sharing sessions.
Community festivals and juggling gatherings in the Bay Area.

WHEN WE MEET
Regular weekly meetings at UC Berkeley.
Events and festivals throughout the year, including larger community gatherings.

WHO WE ARE
A rotating community of students, alumni, and local jugglers.
Volunteer organizers who coordinate meetings, events, and site updates.

THIS WEBSITE
Maintained by members of the Berkeley Juggling Club.
Used to share event information, archives of past festivals, photos, and club resources.
