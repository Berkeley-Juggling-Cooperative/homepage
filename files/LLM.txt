# Berkeley Juggling Club

https://berkeleyjuggling.org

## Overview

The Berkeley Juggling Club is a community-based juggling and circus arts group in Berkeley, California.
We host weekly juggling meetings, public events, and festivals, and maintain an archive of performances and community activity.

## What this site contains

- Weekly juggling meeting information
- Event and festival announcements
- Photo galleries and performance archives
- Community resources related to juggling
- Historical records of club activities and shows
- Juggling patterns

## Audience

This site is intended for:
- Jugglers of all skill levels (beginner to advanced)
- UC Berkeley students and local community members
- Visitors interested in juggling, circus arts, or flow arts events

## Content characteristics

- Event information is time-sensitive and may change each academic term
- Many pages document past events and should be interpreted as historical records
- Media content (photos/videos) reflects community performances and gatherings
- Juggling patterns including SVG files of causal diagrams and position diagrams
