A more complex version of [Torture
Chamber](link:///patterns/torture_chamber) where two people are
getting tortuted at the same time and everyone outside is doing a
sweep.

The pattern is otherwise similar to the static version, but one center
person is going clockwise and the other counterclockwise.

Juggler A
:  Starts with B and goes anti-clockwise

Juggler B
:  Starts with A and goes left, always passes to A in the middle (but catches from F too)

Juggler C
:  Starts with D and goes left, always passes to A in the middle

Juggler D
:  Starts with E and goes left, always passes to F in the middle (but catches from A too)

Juggler E
:  Starts with F and goes left, always passes to F in the middle

Juggler F
:  Starts with C and goes clockwise

In the pattern two people (one in the middle) always exchange clubs
direclty, the other jugglers pass in a large triangle.
