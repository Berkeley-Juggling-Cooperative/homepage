This is a 5 person feast. Everyone is doing 4 count. Each person passes to each person starting with someone and then going to the next person on the right in the circle. This includes parsing to yourself, so to speak, where you just do a normal self.

This pattern can easily be extended to N jugglers. One just still goes around passing to the next juggler to the right.

One an also easily do this in different counts, e.g. 2-, 3-, and 4-count are typical, but really anything works.
