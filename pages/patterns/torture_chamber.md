A 5 person pattern with different difficult levels. It involves two
people doing an 8-count, two people doing a windscreenwiper feed and
one person being tortured in the middle. The patterns in cludes a
backdrop for the person in the middle and for some passes where you
catch from another person than you are passing to with some odd
angles. Passes are normally done in 4-count. There are 4 passes until
the pattern repeats, so counting them out can help.

Everyone starts with 2 clubs in their right hand and one in their left.

Here is what each person does:

Juggler A
:  In the center position: passes to everyone going anti-clockwise,
   including an extrem through to the left while catching from the
   right. Faces juggler B.

Juggler B
:  In one of the two 'front' position. 4-count feed starting with A in
   the center and then going left.

Juggler C
:  Doing an 8-count, always passing to B on 2 and 4.

Juggler D
:  Doing an 8-count, always passing to E on 1 and 3.

Juggler E
:  The other 'front' position. 4-count feed starting with D in
   the center and then going right.
