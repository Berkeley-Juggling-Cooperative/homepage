# Berkeley Jam Spring Fling 2026

We’re excited to host another community-led Juggling & Circus Arts
gathering in the park. Bring your props (or borrow ours) and get ready
to fling, toss & spin them around.

<div class="alert alert-warning text-center" role="alert"> 🌧️
<strong>Weather Update:</strong> Due to heavy rain on Saturday, we
will meet at our covered campus location (see map below). This is
still an <strong>outdoor event</strong> (just with a roof), so please
dress warmly and be prepared for cooler temperatures. 🌬️ </div>

We’re keeping a close eye on conditions and **will be there
regardless**—and we’d love to see anyone who feels up for
juggling despite the weather!

Please note: in our covered campus space, it will be a bit tighter
than in the park if it is still raining tomorrow, so we might need to
share space and be flexible with activities depending on the weather.

The forecast is currently improving, so we’re hopeful for good
juggling conditions either way. Check this page for any last-minute
updates before heading out!

{{% map map_id="dry" lat=37.87085 lng=-122.262 zoom=17 popup="Berkeley Jam Spring Fling ☔🌦️🌦️" height="250px" %}}

What: Completely Free Juggling & Circus Arts Jam

When: Sunday April 12, 11am-5ish

Where: UC Berkeley campus (west side of the new Undergraduate Academic Building which is also at the southside entrace of the Valley Life Sciences building)

Who: All ages and skill levels welcome

We will have workshops and other fun activities lined up throughout the day.

If you have any questions, are interested in helping out, or would like to teach a workshop, please reach out (<festival.info@berkeleyjuggling.org>).
