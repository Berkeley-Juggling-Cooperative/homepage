# Berkeley Jam [ <img src="/images/instagram-logo.png" height=20/> ](https://www.instagram.com/berkeleyjam/)[ <img src="/images/facebook.png" height=20/> ](https://www.facebook.com/events/755384887401754/)

Join us for a Day of Juggling & Circus Arts in the park on September
21nd 2025, to celebrate community and creativity on the heels of
summer transitioning to autumn.

{{% thumbnail "/images/BerkeleyJam_EventInfo.png" align="right" %}}{{% /thumbnail %}}

## When: Sunday 9/21/2025 12-5pm

## Where: Live Oak Park, 1301 Shattuck Ave., Berkeley, CA 94709

{{% map lat=37.88426 lng=-122.26815 zoom=17 popup="Festival location" height="300px" width="300px" float="right" %}}

Whether you're an experienced juggler or an absolute beginner, this
event is for YOU. We're kicking off a fresh chapter in our juggling
community, bringing back the spirit of our past festivals in a casual,
one-day gathering. We’ll be offering free workshops throughout the
day, so come learn a new skill or just enjoy watching the fun. All
ages and skill levels are welcome—no experience necessary! Get ready
for a day of juggling, laughter, and new connections.

## Workshops

Balance Workshop with Zeej: Get ready to wobble, wiggle, and hopefully
anti-climactically just keep balancing in this beginner friendly
balance workshop. We’ll explore the intersection of balance and
juggling, using your body as the foundation for creative expression as
you test balance points from top to toe.

2 Club Shenanigans Workshop with Elan: Take a break from your normal
juggling behavior and come mess around with two clubs. We'll play
around with simple throws, body throws, balancing clubs, as well as
two club trap play. No prerequisites needed.

Kino Prerequisites & Variations Workshop with Kai: Come explore and
deep dive into Kinos, a staple in the “West Coast/Flow” juggling
style. We’ll work through beginner centric drills, improvement
techniques and variations for added flare.

Funky Numbers Passing Patterns with Isaac: Time to get funky and
explore different club passing patterns.

Beginners Guide to Juggling with Mike: This beginner workshop will
teach the foundations of ball juggling, focusing on techniques and
drills to begin your juggle journey.
