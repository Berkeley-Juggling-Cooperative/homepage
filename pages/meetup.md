# New Location(s) !!

We meet every Wednesday at 6pm on the UC Berkeley campus.
This is an outdoor location, so it might be a bit cold depending on the time of the year ;).

We are currently (as of spring 2026) trying out new locations, so make
sure you check this web page in case you cannot find us, since the
locations might have changed. We will keep this page up to date!

We now have three locations: We will be meeting at the Undergraduate
Academic Building and hope to make this out new location independent
of the weather. The meeting location is on the west side of the
building. If we are not there, please check Dwinelle Hall when the
weather is nice and a breezeway at Stephens Hall when it's raining.
